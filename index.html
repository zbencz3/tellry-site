Talebird.

Bedtime stories, made for your kids.

TaleBird is an iOS app that writes personal bedtime stories starring your own children — in classic worlds like Peter Rabbit and the Hundred Acre Wood — and reads them aloud in a warm human voice. Built for the back seat and the bedside.

Starring your own kids.

Personal stories set in classic worlds — the Hundred Acre Wood, Peter Rabbit, Alice in Wonderland, Camelot, Huckleberry Finn's river, and forty more public-domain worlds, grouped by age.

As long as you like.

Pick 5, 10, 15, or 20 minutes. Longer tales unfold as connected little adventures, threaded together.

Read aloud, warmly.

Distinct narration voices in English and Hungarian. Every story grows a soft watercolor cover for the lock screen and Dynamic Island.

Ready offline, before you go.

Prepare the whole playlist ahead of time so every story plays instantly with no signal. Longer stories keep generating in the background — even after you close the app.

Made for the back seat.

A playlist per child, autoplay across stories, and clean Bluetooth playback — play, pause, and skip from the car's own controls.
